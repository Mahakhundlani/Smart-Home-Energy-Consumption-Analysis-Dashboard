## ğŸ” Key Insights
- Night and morning hours show the highest energy consumption, indicating continuous appliance usage.
- Low-load appliances collectively consume nearly 70% of total energy due to frequent and prolonged usage.
- Air conditioners and heaters are the dominant contributors to energy consumption.
- Energy usage remains relatively stable across seasons, highlighting behavioral impact over seasonal variation.
- Extreme weather conditions increase energy demand due to heating and cooling appliances.
